Welcome to Helping Hands


Helping hands is an online and offline educational tool to help develop students skills using a variety of quizes available.
The student may select their grade and start a quiz. There are 3 levels of difficulty Easy, Medium and Hard.
After each quiz you will be shown your score, how many gold stars you can get?


Why we developed Helping Hands


As a group we wanted to help develop a world-class educational tool for
students with special needs to help them acquire the skills they need to excel in life.
We have designed the application to be as user friendly as possible for the students, parents and education staff.
Parents will be able to help students with tests and quizes. They can view the students marks at any time.
Parents can update their childs profile with contact information.
